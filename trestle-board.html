A Message from the Worshipful Master

2026: Building Our Future

Brothers,

This year, we are focused on building a stronger foundation and renewing our purpose as a Masonic Order. My plan moves us clearly toward our core goals: supporting one another, serving our community and elders, and guiding our youth.

This important work takes time. But by staying committed to the path ahead, we will meet all of our goals and obligations. This year, we will carry out clear duties through our 501c3, increase our service efforts, and welcome quality new members.

I look forward to seeing the results of our hard work.

I AM,
Ronnie J. Anderson, Worshipful Master

The Working Tools of a Junior Warden

The Plumb is an instrument used by operative masons to raise perpendiculars. As speculative Masons, we are taught to use it symbolically to walk uprightly in our several stations before God and man, squaring our actions by the Square of Virtue.

Let us each take time this month to reflect on how we maintain our moral plumb line in daily life, ensuring our conduct remains upright and true to our Masonic obligations.
